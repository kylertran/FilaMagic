Welcome to FilaMagic!

Your ultimate platform for managing, organizing, and optimizing your 3D printer filament collection. Created by a dedicated software developer who understands the needs of both hobbyists and professionals, FilaMagic is designed to revolutionize how you manage your 3D printing resources.

Features

  • Track Your Collection: Easily add new filaments to your digital inventory, categorize them by color, brand, location, and more.
  • Calculate Cost of Prints: Upload a 3D model or simply enter the weight of your print, then select the filament you're using to get an accurate cost calculation.
  • Filament Management: Check off which filament is currently loaded into your printer and note down the ideal temperatures and flow rates.
  • Advanced Color Picker: Choose from a wide range of colors to accurately represent your filament.
  • Batch Import/Export: Import or export your filament data in bulk via CSV, JSON, or PDF files.
  • User Statistics: View insightful statistics like total filament used, most-used filament, and total cost of prints.
  • Dark Mode: Switch to dark mode for a more comfortable user experience.

Practical Applications

Whether you're a 3D printing hobbyist or a seasoned professional, FilaMagic offers a range of benefits:

  • Inventory Management: Keep track of your filament stock and avoid unnecessary purchases.
  • Cost Efficiency: Calculate the cost of your prints to manage your 3D printing budget effectively.
  • Digital Catalog: Create a digital catalog of your filament collection for insurance purposes or to have a backup.
